## What is RAG and it use?

RAG (Retrieval-Augmented Generation) is an AI technique that improves the accuracy of Large Language Models (LLMs) by providing relevant information from external documents before generating an answer.

RAG combines two steps:

1.Retrieval – Searches a knowledge base or vector database and finds the most relevant document chunks related to the user's question.

2.Generation – Sends the retrieved information as context to an LLM (such as Gemini, Groq, or GPT), which generates a response based on that context.

Instead of answering only from the model’s internal training knowledge, RAG allows the AI to use external, up-to-date, and domain-specific information.
